Looking To Adopt

We take great pride in offering adoption services. We have a well built reputation with years of experience placing thousands of children in adoptive families.If you are planning on adoption or need adoption advice, we will handle your adoption in a professional, confidential and ethical manner.

Considering Adoption Agency provides a full-service Infant Adoption Program for adoptive families in California, including the initial home study.

Considering Adoption Agency also offers the following services:

  • Waiting Child home studies (for families interested in adopting a child in state custody)
  • Domestic infant home studies
  • Post-placement reports
  • Birth parent contract services (counseling and relinquishments for expectant parents. Contact us for more info.)
  • Post-adoption counseling
  • Connections search and reunion program (for adult adoptees and birth parents looking to reunite)
